Key Challenges in Aquaculture

The fish farming industry in Bangladesh faces critical issues:

  • •
    Unsafe Lighting Methods:Over 4.7 million ponds still use 220V bulbs, posing serious electrical hazards.
  • •
    High Energy Costs:Farmers rely on grid electricity, increasing operational costs.
  • •
    Resource Wastage:Traditional lights attract few insects and contribute to feed waste.
  • •
    Environmental Risk:Electric short circuits cause pond contamination and fish mortality.
  • •
    Scalability Barriers:Current tools are not designed for easy scaling in rural settings.
